Article 72 Start LAW of operations 1 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall determine the date from which the ESP is to start operations by means of an implementing act once the following conditions have been met: (a) the measures referred to in Articles 8(2), 9(7 CARDINAL ) and 43(5) have been adopted; (b) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of a comprehensive test of the ESP, which it has conducted in cooperation with the Member States GPE authorities and the Union agencies that may use the ESP; (c) eu-LISA PERSON has validated the technical and legal arrangements to collect and transmit the data referred to in Article 8(1 LAW ) and has notified them to the Commission ORG . The ESP shall only query the Interpol ORG databases once the technical arrangements allow compliance with Article 9(5 LAW ). Any impossibility of complying with Article 9(5 LAW ) shall have the result that the ESP does not query the Interpol ORG databases but shall not delay the start of operations of the ESP. The Commission ORG shall set the date referred to in the first ORDINAL subparagraph to be within 30 days DATE from adoption of the implementing act. 2 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall determine the date from which the shared BMS ORG is to start operations by means of an implementing act once the following conditions have been met: (a) the measures referred to in Articles GPE 13(5) and 43(5) have been adopted; (b) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of a comprehensive test of the shared BMS ORG , which it has conducted in cooperation with the Member States GPE authorities; (c) eu-LISA PERSON has validated the technical and legal arrangements to collect and transmit the data referred to in Article 13 LAW and has notified them to the Commission ORG ; (d) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of the test referred to in paragraph 5(b CARDINAL ). The Commission shall set the date referred to in the first ORDINAL subparagraph to be within 30 days DATE from adoption of the implementing act. 3 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall determine the date from which the CIR ORG is to start operations by means of an implementing act once the following conditions have been met: (a) the measures referred to in Articles LOC 43(5) and 78(10) have been adopted; (b) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of a comprehensive test of the CIR ORG , which it has conducted in cooperation with the Member States GPE authorities; (c) eu-LISA PERSON has validated the technical and legal arrangements to collect and transmit the data referred to in Article 18 LAW and has notified them to the Commission ORG ; (d) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of the test referred to in paragraph 5(b CARDINAL ). The Commission shall set the date referred to in the first ORDINAL subparagraph to be within 30 days DATE from adoption of the implementing act. 4 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall determine the date from which the MID ORG is to start operations by means of an implementing act once the following conditions have been met: (a) the measures referred to in Articles 28(5 GPE ) and (7), 32(5), 33(6 CARDINAL ), 43(5) and 49(6 CARDINAL ) have been adopted; (b) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of a comprehensive test of the MID ORG , which it has conducted in cooperation with the Member States GPE authorities and the ETIAS Central Unit; (c) eu-LISA PERSON has validated the technical and legal arrangements to collect and transmit the data referred to in Article 34 LAW and has notified them to the Commission ORG ; (d) the ETIAS Central Unit has notified the Commission ORG in accordance with Article 71(3 LAW ); (e) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of the tests referred to in paragraphs 1(b CARDINAL ), 2(b CARDINAL ), 3(b CARDINAL ) and 5(b CARDINAL ). The Commission shall set the date referred to in the first ORDINAL subparagraph to be within 30 days DATE from adoption of the implementing act. 5 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall determine by means of implementing acts the date from which the automated data quality control mechanisms and procedures, the common data quality indicators and the minimum data quality standards are to be used, once the following conditions have been met: (a) the measures referred to in Articles PERSON 37(4) have been adopted; (b) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of a comprehensive test of the automated data quality control mechanisms and procedures, the common data quality indicators and the minimum data quality standards, which it has conducted in cooperation with the Member States GPE authorities. The Commission shall set the date referred to in the first ORDINAL subparagraph to be within 30 days DATE from adoption of the implementing act. 6 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall determine the date from which the CRRS ORG is to start operations by means of an implementing act once the following conditions have been met: (a) the measures referred to in Articles PERSON 39(5) and 43(5) have been adopted; (b) eu-LISA PERSON has declared the successful completion of a comprehensive test of the CRRS ORG , which it has conducted in cooperation with the Member States GPE authorities; (c) eu-LISA PERSON has validated the technical and legal arrangements to collect and transmit the data referred to in Article 39 LAW and has notified them to the Commission. The Commission shall set the date referred to in the first ORDINAL subparagraph to be within 30 days DATE from adoption of the implementing act. 7 CARDINAL . The Commission ORG shall inform the European Parliament ORG and the Council of the ORG results of the tests carried out pursuant to paragraphs 1(b CARDINAL ), 2(b CARDINAL ), 3(b CARDINAL ), 4(b CARDINAL ), 5(b CARDINAL ) and 6(b CARDINAL ). 8 CARDINAL . Member States GPE , the ETIAS Central Unit ORG and Europol ORG shall start using each of the interoperability components from the date determined by the Commission ORG in accordance with paragraphs 1 CARDINAL , 2 DATE , 3 CARDINAL and 4 DATE respectively.
